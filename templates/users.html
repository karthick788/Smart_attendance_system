Register New User
To register a new user, you need to use the command line tool.
Step 1: Open Command Prompt/Terminal

Navigate to your project directory and run:

python register_user.py
Step 2: Follow the Interactive Prompts
  • Enter the user's name
  • Enter email (optional)
  • Enter department (optional)
  • Position yourself in front of the camera
  • Press SPACE to capture face samples (10 samples recommended)
Alternative: Command Line Arguments

You can also provide information directly:

python register_user.py --name "John Doe" --email "john@example.com" --department "IT"
Tips for best results:
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Look directly at the camera
  • Capture samples from different angles
  • Avoid wearing glasses or hats if possible
